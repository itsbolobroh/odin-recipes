Lasagna

Description

See below for easy to bake lasagna! This is the perfect meal for that holiday party you are hosting!See below for a list of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Beef
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Place Lasagna on baking tray and bake for 90 minutes
  3. Remove Lasagna from tray and enjoy your meal!
