Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

This is a straightforward recipe to bake fantastic chocolate chip cookies in under 45 minutes. There are only a few ingredients neccessary, and all can be purchased at your local supermarket! See below for the official list.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsps vanilla
  • 1/2 tsps salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
